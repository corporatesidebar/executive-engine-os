2. Confirm the app loads.
3. Confirm all four columns are visible on desktop width.

5. Confirm main command area remains in the approved position.

7. Confirm POST goes to https://executive-engine-os.onrender.com/run.
8. Confirm user message appears as a right-side bubble.
9. Confirm assistant response appears as a left-side guided workflow card.
10. Confirm response starts with a plain-English answer.
11. Confirm “Do this next” appears.
12. Confirm action steps render as task rows.
13. Confirm ready assets render as asset cards.
14. Confirm risk renders as a warning card.
15. Confirm “Continue with recommended command” executes a follow-up in the same thread.
16. Confirm sidebar items display runtime workspace data.
17. Confirm Executive Summary updates from runtime state.
18. Confirm Executive Intelligence updates from runtime state.
19. Confirm no backend files are included.
20. Confirm API URL and /run contract were not changed.
