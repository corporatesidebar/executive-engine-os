Executive Engine OS - Executive Flow Product Vision

PRODUCT THESIS
The AI box is not the product. The prepared executive day is the product.

CORE SHIFT
From command/chat-first dashboard to push-first Executive Flow dashboard.
The executive should not need to think of prompts all day.
The system should push what matters: today, tomorrow, meetings, deals, proposals, decisions, people, risks, files, and next actions.

MAIN DASHBOARD
- Today
- Tomorrow
- Meetings
- Revenue / Deals
- Decisions
- People
- Risks / Constraints
- Prepared For You
- End of Day

INPUT ROLE
The input is small and thin at the top.
It captures light context: "What needs to happen?"
The dashboard is the product, not the input box.

UNIVERSAL TASK CAPTURE
Who - person, company, client, team, contractor, candidate.
What - what needs to happen.
When - date, time, deadline.
Where - call, Zoom, office, email, client site.
Why - revenue, risk, relationship, hiring, firing, operations, growth.
How - proposal, deck, agenda, email, brief, follow-up, contract, file.

OPERATING FLOW
Night before:
"Tomorrow I have a meeting with ABC HVAC about marketing. Need SEO, ads, Instagram, and maybe a proposal."

Morning:
"Good morning Will - you're covered. ABC HVAC meeting prep is ready. Proposal angle drafted. Questions ready. Follow-up email ready. Decision needed: confirm offer/package."

TONE
Short. Confident. Supportive. Personal. Executive-grade.
No weak phrasing.
No "try".
No "can't".
Make the executive feel prepared, in control, and ready to win.

BUILD RULES
Preserve /run, /test-report, V3003 real revenue asset engine.
No Supabase schema change.
No OAuth.
No external writes.
Manual execution only.
Auto-loop off.

V3700 GOAL
Build the Executive Flow Dashboard: a push-first daily operating layer where executives see what matters, what is ready, what needs a decision, and what could go wrong before they ask for anything.
